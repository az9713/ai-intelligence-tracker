Mark is_relevant=false for any of the following:
- Vendor press releases or marketing that contain no concrete datapoints (numbers, dates, named customers, capacity figures, dollar values)
- Paywalled articles where the snippet alone carries no usable information
- Listicles ("top 10 AI tools") or generic explainer articles with no primary data

relevance scoring (0.0–1.0):
- 1.0: Directly reports on the bottleneck layer or industry with primary-source evidence
- 0.5: Indirectly relevant — touches the topic but from an adjacent angle
- 0.2: Weakly relevant — passing mention in a broader story

summary: Write exactly one factual sentence derived strictly from the title and snippet. Do not fabricate details not present in the input. If the snippet is too thin to support a factual sentence, write "Insufficient data in snippet."
